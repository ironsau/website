Irons Family History

In the early 1800's Joseph and his wife Alice Irons reared a family of four sons. They lived in Littleport, Cambridgeshire, England. After Joseph's death in 1844 his youngest son John and his family immigrated to the colony of South Australia, and in 1848 his second brother Turner and his family followed, also as assisted immigrants.

The next Irons Family known to have immigrated to Australia arrived in Melbourne about 140 years later.
